Welcome to our recommendations page!

Madrid is a city that captures your heart from the very first moment. After four years of calling this vibrant capital home, I’ve discovered countless hidden gems and beloved spots that make it truly special. Madrid is a city whose scale sweeps you away at first glance—impressive buildings, grand avenues, and wide boulevards create a sense of elegance and openness. So carefully maintained, so clean, and so full of life, it is impossible not to be moved by its energy. It’s a city that never sleeps.

It may not have an Acropolis or an Eiffel Tower, yet Madrid leaves you speechless in its own way—with deep-rooted culture of art, music, and creativity. Its architecture varies beautifully from neighborhood to neighborhood, each area revealing a completely different personality—so unique and distinctive that you never tire of exploring it. From Baroque façades to contemporary landmarks, from vast green parks to modern skyscrapers, Madrid feels like a captivating blend of Parisian grandeur and London’s cosmopolitan edge or a tiny New York version.
